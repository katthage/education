My Ideal Work-Life Balance

1. A higher salary is more important than working only 30 hours a week.

Reasons to Agree

  • Buy anything I like
  • Go on vacation / travel around the world
  • Insurance for the future
  • Possibility to invest money
  • Better life quality

Reasons to Disagree

  • Less free time for friends and family and hobbies
  • More and harder work
  • Stressful
  • Less time for relaxation

2. I would never want to work in an open-plan office.

Reasons to Agree

  • Open offices are noisy and distracting
  • Lack of privacy
  • Spread of illnesses
  • Harder to concentrate

Reasons to Disagree

  • Fosters collaboration
  • Dynamic environment
  • Better team bonding
  • Easy communication

3. I want to have responsibilities in my job and not have a boss tell me everything.

Reasons to Agree

  • I can make decisions
  • I gain confidence
  • Personal development
  • Leadership skills

Reasons to Disagree

  • Have to make right choices
  • Overwhelming
  • Less free time
  • Stress

4. I do not want to work extra hours or on weekends, even for more pay.

Reasons to Agree

  • Important free time
  • Healthy work-life balance
  • Prevents burnout
  • Time for hobbies and relaxation

Reasons to Disagree

  • Extra pay
  • Career advancement
  • Sense of achievement
  • Recognition from management

5. I prefer to work in a team rather than alone.

Reasons to Agree

  • Exchange information
  • Help from others if I have problems
  • Is more motivating
  • Shared responsibilities

Reasons to Disagree

  • There might be conflicts
  • Shy people cannot express themselves
  • Harder to concentrate
  • Decisions take longer

6. I am willing to accept a high workload for a high salary.

Reasons to Agree

  • Faster career growth
  • Sense of achievement
  • Financial security
  • Opportunity to learn new skills

Reasons to Disagree

  • Less time
  • More stress
  • Impact on health
  • Less time for personal interests

7. Enjoying my life is more important than building a career at work.

Reasons to Agree

  • Life is short, and happiness is key
  • Personal experiences can be more rewarding
  • Enjoying life can reduce stress
  • Better mental health

Reasons to Disagree

  • A career can provide long-term security
  • Achievements at work can be fulfilling
  • Building a career can improve your skills
  • Financial stability

8. Having a chance for promotion is better than having a relaxed and easy job.

Reasons to Agree

  • Promotions can lead to better pay
  • It can provide motivation to do your best
  • Advancing in your career can be rewarding
  • More responsibilities and challenges

Reasons to Disagree

  • An easy job can mean less stress
  • You have more time for personal interests
  • It can lead to higher job satisfaction
  • More time for family and friends

9. Spending evenings with family and friends is more important than working extra hours for a promotion.

Reasons to Agree

  • Family and friends bring joy and support
  • Spending time with loved ones creates memories
  • It can lead to a happier life
  • Improves mental health

Reasons to Disagree

  • Working extra can lead to career growth
  • It can provide financial rewards
  • It shows dedication to your job
  • Potential for future promotions

10. Having flexible shifts is better than fixed working hours, even if it means being available on-call at any time.

Reasons to Agree

  • Flexible shifts can fit personal schedules
  • It allows you to work when you are most productive
  • You can better balance work and life
  • Accommodates unexpected events

Reasons to Disagree

  • Fixed hours provide a stable routine
  • It sets clear work-life boundaries
  • There is less stress about being on-call
  • Predictable schedule
